\n Crow feet sign (round atelectasis)\n

\n
\n
\n
\n
\n \nCrow feet sign is a characteristic, but uncommon, feature seen in round atelectasis.\n\nOn CT, this is seen as linear bands radiating from a mass into adjacent lung tissue resembling the feet of a crow. This sign should not be confused with fibrotic changes occurring in the lung.\n\n
